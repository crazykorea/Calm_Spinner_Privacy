Paid Random Item Probability Disclosure

If the App offers paid random draws for buffs or skins, the following probability structure applies.

Buff Draw Probability Structure

Buff draws are determined from the pool of remaining, not-yet-obtained buffs. Each buff has a weight based on its rarity tier, and the actual probability is calculated from the total weight of all remaining candidate buffs.

Current weight values:

  • Common: 4
  • Higher/Common-advanced tier: 3
  • Rare: 2
  • Legendary: 1
  • Special: 0.5

The actual probability of receiving a specific buff depends on the player’s current remaining buff pool. For example, if the remaining pool contains:

  • 2 Common buffs (weight 4 each)
  • 1 Rare buff (weight 2)
  • 1 Legendary buff (weight 1)
  • 1 Special buff (weight 0.5)

Then the total weighted value is 2×4 + 1×2 + 1×1 + 1×0.5 = 11.5.

The values below are shown as: count ratio in the remaining pool (weighted probability)

  • Common tier: 2 / 5 (8 / 11.5)
  • Rare tier: 1 / 5 (2 / 11.5)
  • Legendary tier: 1 / 5 (1 / 11.5)
  • Special tier: 1 / 5 (0.5 / 11.5)

Because both buff draws and skin draws depend on the current remaining candidate pool, the exact weighted probability of each item changes as more items are obtained.
